Pumpkin Pie Smothies

Requirements-you will need 1 can (15 ounces) pumpkin chilled, 1-1/3 cups evaporated milk (about 10 ounces) chilled, 1 cup orange juice, 1/3 cup packed brown sugar, 1/2 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice, 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon, 1 small ripe banana, cut into thirds.
Recipe
Process all ingredients in a blender until smooth.Then put it in a jar or glass to pour the smoothie in. 256 calories, 6g fat , 46g carbohydrate , 7g protein.
Disclaimer: Never leave a child unattended in the kitchen. Limit the child to tasks that are safe and age-appropriate.
